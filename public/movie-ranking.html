My top 3 movies of all-time.

Training Day

A newly recruited police officer spends his first day as a narcotics officer with a detective who is more than what he appears to be.
Release date: October 5, 2001

The Departed

An undercover agent and a spy constantly try to counter-attack each other in order to save themselves from being exposed in front of the authorities. Meanwhile, both try to infiltrate an Irish gang.
Release date: October 6, 2006

The Dark Knight

After Gordon, Dent and Batman begin an assault on Gotham's organised crime, the mobs hire the Joker, a psychopathic criminal mastermind who offers to kill Batman and bring the city to its knees.
Release date: July 18, 2008
